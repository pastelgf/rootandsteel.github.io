# Melee Weapons

# Standard

## Fists

*Special:* You only have access to this weapon if you have an open readied slot.

**Strike:** basic strike vs PD, 1 damage, +1 damage on hit

**Haymaker:** 1 xy, strike vs PD, 2 damage, push 1 on hit

**Flury of Blows:** 2 xy, make a basic fist strike against all adjacent enemies.

## Straight Sword

**Strike:** basic strike vs PD, 2 damage, +2 damage and bleed 2 on hit

**Pommel Strike:** 1 xy, strike vs PD, 1 damage, daze 1 on hit

**Parry:** 2 xy, use when hit by an attack, strike vs 8, reduce damage by 2, +2 damage reduction on hit. If damage is reduced ot 0, make a free basic strike.

## Thrusting Sword

**Strike:** basic strike vs PD, 2 damage, +2 damage on hit, +2 damage if youâ€™ve moved this round.

**Blade Waltz:** 1 xy, Strike vs PD, move 2 then 2 damage, +2 damage on hit

**Dancing Stance:** 2 xy, stance, the first time each round you move adjacent to an enemy, you may basic strike them for free.

## Curved Sword

**Strike:** basic strike vs PD, 2 damage, +2 damage on hit, gain haste 1

**Wind Blade:** 1 xy, 3 wide line, strike vs PD, 2 damage, +2 damage on hit

**Retreating Blow:** 1 xy, strike vs PD, 2 damage and shift back up to 1, +1 on hit, shift distance is increased by haste

## Dagger

**Strike:** basic strike vs PD, 1 damage, +2 damage on hit, +2 damage if an ally is adjacent to the target

**Cloak and Dagger:** 1 xy, strike vs PD, 2 damage, +1 damage and gain hidden 2 on hit

**Flash Step:** 2 xy, dash through an adjacent target then make a basic strike against it. You may repeat this any number of times, but only once against any target.

## Axe

**Strike:** basic strike vs PD, 2 damage, +3 on hit

**Hack:** 1 xy, strike vs PD, 2 damage, +1 damage and hobbled 2 on hit

**Execute:** 1 xy, strike vs PD, 2 damage, +2 damage on hit, automatically kills if target is left with 5 or less hitpoints

## Mace

**Strike:** basic strike vs PD, 2 damage, shattered 2 on hit

**Bash:** 1 xy, strike vs PD, 2 damage and push 1, push 1 on hit

**Skull Cracker:** 2 xy, strike vs PD, 3 damage and knock prone, +2 damage on hit

## Flail

**Strike:** basic attack vs 6, 2 damage, +1 damage on hit

**Sweeping Chain:** 1 xy, 3 wide line, strike vs 6, knock prone, 1 damage on hit

**Twin Whirl:** 2 xy, strike vs 6, 2 damage, +2 damage on hit, repeats after use (can target different enemies)

## Spear

**Strike:** 2 range, basic strike vs PD, 2 damage, +1 damage on hit

**Impale:** 1 xy, line 2, strike vs PD, 2 damage, bleeding 2 on hit

**Spearpoint Stance:** 2 xy, stance, you may attack of opportunity creatures that move adjacent to you. If you hit at least once, also push 1.

## Greatsword

**Strike:** basic strike vs PD, 2 damage, +3 damage on hit

**Charge:** 1 xy, 6 range shift, Strike vs PD, 1 damage per tile shifted, +2 on hit

**The Ninth Rule:** 1 xy, Strike vs PD, 3 damage, gain reinforced 1 for one turn on hit. You can grant this reinforced to an adjacent ally instead.

**Overwhelming Stance:** 2 xy, stance, all of your attacks gain an additional hit (regardless if they scored any hits). 

## Greataxe

**Strike:** basic attack vs PD, 2 damage, +3 damage on hit

**Cleqave:** 1 xy, 3 wide line, attack vs PD, 2 damage, +3 damage on hit

**Split Man:** 2 xy, attack vs PD, 3 damage, +3 damage on hit, deals damage twice

**Whirlwind** 2 xy, burst 3, 2 damage, =2 damage on hit

## Maul

**Strike:** basic strike vs PD, 2 damage, +1 damage and shattered 1 on hit

**Thump:** 1 xy, strike vs PD, 2 damage, +2 damage on hit, damage doubled against prone targets

**Skull Knock:** 1 xy, strike vs PD, 2 damage, daze 1 on hit

**Hammer Down:** 2 xy, cone 3, 3 damage, +2 damage on hit

## Quarterstaff

Whirling Blows X: Increases hits of basic quarterstaff strikes by X. Decays fully at end of round.

**Strike:** basic strike vs PD, 2 damage, +2 damage on hit.

**Dust Storm:** 1 xy, cone 3, strike vs PD, blind 2 and gain whirling blows 2, blind 1 and gain whirling blows 1 on hit

**Thwack:** 1 xy, range 2, strike vs PD, 3 damage and gain whirling blows 2, +2 damage on hit

**Nine Strikes Stance:** 2 xy, stance, you cannot make basic attacks. At the end of the round gain whirling blows 2 and make a free basic strike.

# Shields

## Buckler

**Strike:** basic strike or shoot vs PD, 1 damage, push 1 on hit

**Shields Up:** 1 xy, use when attacked, strike or shoot vs 8, reduce your damage taken by 1, +2 damage reduction on hit

## Medium Shield

**Strike:** basic strike vs PD, 1 damage, +1 damage and push 1 on hit

**Shields Up:** 1 xy, use when attacked, strike vs 8, reduce your damage taken by 2, +2 damage reduction on hit

**Shield Slam:** 2 xy, strike vs PD, 2 damage and daze 1, daze 1 on hit

## Tower Shield

**Strike:** basic strike vs PD, push 2, 1 damage on hit

**Mighty Slam:** 1 xy, cone 2, strike vs PD, 2 damage, gain shield 2 on hit

**Inspiring Bash:** 1 xy, burst 5, all allies in range gain shield 3, strength 1, and focus 1

**Shields Up:** 2 xy, use when attacked, strike vs 8, reduce your damage taken by 3, +2 damage reduction on hit

# Light Sources

## Torch

**Strike:** basic strike vs PD, 1 damage and reduce the torch's light level by 1, +2 damage and burning 1 on hit

**Snuffing Strike:** 1 xy, strike vs PD, 2 damage and burning 2, +2 damage and burning 1 on hit, the torch goes out.

## Lantern

**Strike:** basic attack vs PD, 0 damage, +1 damage on hit

**Shatter:** 1 xy, burst 3, all open tiles become burning tile effects, the lantern is consumed

# Exotic

## Firelance

*Loading:* After firing the firelance, it must be reloaded to use certain actions. Starts combat loaded.

**Strike:** basic strike vs PD, 2 damage, +2 damage on hit, can fire the firelance for +3 damage

**Shoot:** 6 range, basic shoot vs PD, 2 damage, +2 damage on hit, fires the firelance

**Reload:** Reloads the firelance

**Bullet Arc:** 1 xy, 3 wide line, strike vs PD, 2 damage, +1 damage on hit, can fire the firelance for +2 damage

**Fientfire:** 2 xy, shoot vs PD, 2 damage and shift three tiles away from the target, +2 damage on hit, firelance must be loaded to use

## Whip

**Strike:** range 3, basic strike vs PD, 1 damage and move the target 1, move the target 1 more on hit

**Brutal Lash:** 1 xy, range 3, strike vs PD, 2 damage and bleed 3, +1 damage and bleed 2 on hit

**Disarm:** 1 xy, range 3, strike vs PD, weaken 3, weaken 2 on hit

## Twinblade

Dancing Steel X: At three stacks, all stacks are cleansed and you make a free basic twinblade strike.

**Strike:** basic attack vs PD, 2 damage, +2 on hit, gain dancing steel 1

**Whirling Blade:** 1 xy, attack vs PD, move 1 and then all enemies in burst 2 take 2 damage, +2 on hit, gain dancing steel 1

**Blade Shield:** 1 xy, attack vs PD, 1 damage and gain shield 2, +1 damage and gain shield 1 on hit, gain dancing steel 1

**Piercing Blade:** 2 xy, range 2, attack vs PD, 3 damage then move up to 3, +2 damage on hit, gain dancing steel 1

## Scythe

**Strike:** basic strike vs PD, deal 1 damage and bleed 1, +2 damage on hit

**Yank:** 1 xy, cone 2, strike vs PD, deal 2 damage and pull 1, +2 damage on hit

**Reap:** 2 xy, 3 wide line, strike vs PD, deal 3 damage, +2 damage on hit, gain an additional hit for each enemy hit past the first

**Guillotine:** 2 xy, strike vs PD, 3 damage, +2 damage on hit, all bleed is cleansed to do damage equal to its value, automatically kills if target is left with 5 or less hitpoints
